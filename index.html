Features

Engineered for connection.

Pixel-Perfect Sync

When you scroll, they scroll. Instantly. No more "Wait, which paragraph are you on?"

Emotional Overlay

Double-tap anywhere to react. See your friend's laughter or shock appear right on the text.

Whisper Chat

A minimalist sidebar chat that doesn't cover your content. Fade it out when you need focus.

Universal Formats
